About MoLenerzi

Empowering SMEs with smart, accessible, and climate-focused digital tools.

Our Mission

MoLenerzi is a sustainability-driven platform built to support Small and Medium Enterprises in improving efficiency, reducing waste, cutting emissions, and strengthening resilience. Many SMEs lack the tools or resources to measure their environmental footprint or operate efficiently — we aim to change that with simple, AI-powered solutions.

What We Provide

Our platform integrates digital tools that work both online and offline, ensuring accessibility even in disaster-prone or low-connectivity regions. We help SMEs:

  • Monitor and reduce their carbon footprint using guided audits.
  • Optimize energy use through smart recommendations.
  • Trade recyclable or reusable waste via a circular economy marketplace.
  • Submit VAT returns securely through encrypted offline SMS.
  • Access green financing opportunities to adopt sustainable practices.

Our Vision

We believe sustainability should be simple, affordable, and accessible to everyone, especially small businesses. MoLenerzi combines AI, mobile technologies, and inclusive design to support SDG 13 (Climate Action) and build a greener, more resilient future for businesses in Mauritius and beyond.
